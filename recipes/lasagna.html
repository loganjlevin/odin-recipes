Lasagna

Description

The best lasagna you will ever make from home!

Simple ingredients, simple recipes, extraordinary flavor!

Ingredients

  • Sheet pasta
  • Pasta sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Parmesean cheese

Steps

  1. Boil sheet pasta
  2. Cook ground beef
  3. Add pasta sauce
  4. Construct lasagna by layering: Sauce, pasta, parmesean cheese.
  5. Repeat step 4 until ingredients are gone.
  6. Bake at 375 F for 45 minutes.
