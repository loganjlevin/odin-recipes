Chili

Description

The best chili you will ever make from home!

Simple ingredients, simple recipes, extraordinary flavor!

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Diced onions
  • Bacon
  • Beans
  • chili powder

Steps

  1. In large pot over medium heat combine tomato sauce, tomatoes, onion.
  2. Cook bacon, then add to pot.
  3. Add beans and chili powder/
  4. Reduce heat and let simmer stiring occasionally until flavors are well blended.
