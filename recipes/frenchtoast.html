French Toast

Description

The best French Toast you will ever make from home!

Simple ingredients, simple recipes, extraordinary flavor!

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Measure flour into mixing large mixing bowl. Whisk in the milk, salt, egss, sugar until smooth.
  2. Heat lightly oiled frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Soak bread slices in mixure. Cook bread on each side until golden brown.
